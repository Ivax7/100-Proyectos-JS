Cosmere Timeline

Apart from the reading order (which as we have mentioned you can follow or not) Brandon has been providing over time the chronological order in which the Cosmere books take place. If you still don't know what the cosmere is, we recommend that you also visit our section on what the cosmere is. Keep in mind that despite being an official order, the time spaces between these sagas are not completely defined and can always change or new sagas appear in between, so we recommend that you revisit this section from time to time if you are curious about the subject. One of the questions we read the most is where a saga is temporarily located compared to the rest, this is accentuated when we start to have several eras within the same series. In this timeline we only place the stories to simplify but it will resolve general doubts regarding chronological order.

Dragonsteel

Although it has not yet been published and will be one of the last sagas to see the light, "Dragonsteel" tells the events that triggered the Cosmere as we know it and will be set on Yolen, the original planet of humans.

White Sand

Although we do not know the years that separate "White Sand" from "Dragonsteel," this work familiarizes us with some characters who will be relevant in the Cosmere and what we know of this universe.

Elantris

The interesting events and magic (also known as Investiture) of "Elantris" will reverberate into the future.

The Emperor's Soul

"The Emperor's Soul" is a highly recommended short story where it is worth paying attention to the magic system known as Forgery.

Mistborn Era 1

The first era of "Mistborn" (the original trilogy) takes place before the events narrated in "Warbreaker."

Warbreaker

Although this novel is often overlooked, the events in "Warbreaker" will have repercussions over time.

Nightblood

Although not yet written, the book "Nightblood" will take place right after "Warbreaker" and will be another key moment in the Cosmere.

Shadows for Silence in the Forests of Hell

This is another story from the "Arcanum Unbounded" collection and is interesting for exploring Realmatic interconnections.

Arcanum Unbounded

"Arcanum Unbounded" appears at this point not as a collection but as the moment when a scholar starts writing notes to unravel the mysteries of the Cosmere.

The Stormlight Archive Era 1

From here, the first era of "The Stormlight Archive" takes place (books 1 to 5).

Mistborn Era 2

The second era of "Mistborn," which is separated by three hundred years from the events that end with "The Hero of Ages," takes place after the first era of "The Stormlight Archive."

The Stormlight Archive Era 2

We know nothing about the second era of "The Stormlight Archive," except that it will take place after the second era of "Mistborn."

Mistborn Era 3

"Mistborn" Era 3 will be set in a period equivalent to our 1980s, where the main character will be a Terris woman who is a programmer and uses nicrosil, possibly alongside her brother.

Sixth of the Dusk

"Sixth of the Dusk" is a much more important short story than it seems at first glance and has taken us the farthest into the Cosmere so far.

Mistborn Era 4

Speculations about the Cosmere timeline conclude with "Mistborn" Era 4, which will have a space opera setting with faster-than-light travel technology.
